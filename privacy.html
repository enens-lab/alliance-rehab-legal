Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Alliance Rehab ("we", "us", "our"), located at 9555 Warner Ave Suite A, Fountain Valley, CA 92708, collects, uses, and protects information about patients and prospective patients who interact with our clinic by phone, including via our automated phone receptionist system.

1. Information We Collect

When you call Alliance Rehab to book, reschedule, cancel, or inquire about an appointment, we collect:

  • Identifiers: your name, callback phone number.
  • Health-related context: the reason you are calling (e.g., "back pain", "post-surgical knee rehab"). We do not collect medical records, diagnoses, test results, or treatment notes through this channel.
  • Appointment details: requested date, time, and any rescheduling or cancellation activity.
  • Call audio and transcripts: phone calls to our main line may be recorded and transcribed in order to deliver and improve the service.

2. How We Use Information

  • To schedule, confirm, reschedule, or cancel your appointment.
  • To send you a one-time SMS confirmation of your appointment, plus follow-up SMS messages tied to that appointment (rescheduling or cancellation).
  • To deliver internal staff notifications related to call routing and message handling.
  • To maintain operational records required by applicable law and to provide patient care.

3. SMS Messaging Program

If you provide a phone number when booking an appointment, you consent to receive transactional SMS from Alliance Rehab regarding that appointment.

  • Program name: Alliance Rehab Appointment Notifications
  • Message types: appointment confirmation, reschedule confirmation, cancellation confirmation, and operational staff notifications.
  • Message frequency: approximately 1–3 messages per appointment booking.
  • Cost: Message and data rates may apply (charged by your wireless carrier).
  • Opt-out: Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe. You may also call (714) 968-8700 to be removed.
  • Help: Reply HELP for assistance, or call (714) 968-8700.
  • Marketing: We do not use SMS for marketing or promotional purposes.

SMS opt-in information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers acting on our behalf to operate our phone and scheduling systems, under contractual obligations that limit their use of the data:

  • Twilio, Inc. — telephony and SMS message delivery
  • Vapi.ai — voice agent orchestration
  • Google LLC — calendar scheduling (Google Calendar)
  • ElevenLabs, Inc. — text-to-speech audio generation
  • Deepgram, Inc. — speech-to-text transcription

We may also disclose information when required by law, in response to a valid legal request, or to protect the safety of our patients, staff, or the public.

5. Data Retention

We retain appointment records and related information for as long as necessary to deliver care and comply with applicable medical recordkeeping requirements under California and federal law. Call recordings and transcripts used to operate the receptionist service are retained for a limited operational period and then deleted, unless required to be kept longer for legitimate business or legal reasons.

6. Your Choices

  • You may opt out of SMS at any time by replying STOP or by calling the clinic.
  • You may request access to or deletion of your information by contacting us at (714) 968-8700.
  • If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). To exercise those rights, contact us using the information below.

7. Security

We use industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect the information we collect. No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for patients and caregivers booking appointments at Alliance Rehab. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 without parent or guardian involvement.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are effective when posted on this page. The "Effective date" at the top reflects the most recent revision.
